# User Persona & Use Case

---

## Persona: Sarah R., 37
- Owns a home with solar + battery  
- Charges an EV overnight  
- Wants to reduce energy bills  
- Not technically trained  

### Goals
- Monitor solar generation  
- Know when her battery is low  
- Charge EV at cheapest / cleanest time  
- Reduce bill without complex calculations  

---

## Primary Use Case

### Scenario:
Sarah opens the HESS app to decide whether she should charge her EV this afternoon.

### Steps:
1. She checks **solar output**  
2. She views **battery %**  
3. She toggles EV charging ON  
4. AI recommends:  
   - “Solar output exceeds home load — charging now is optimal”  

### Outcome:
She saves money by using excess solar power.
